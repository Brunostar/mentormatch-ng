Why Use Mentor Match?

A mentor should be able to stand for his mentee(s). Vouching for them in technical situations, and them removing every possible obstacle that could be in their way. After the mentee has made a convincing attempt, of course.

Having faith in yourself can make you. But success only comes with hard work and positive thinking. While you search for purpose, keep a positive attitude, have faith in yourself and your mentor.

Confidante

First and foremost, your mentee(s) sees you as a friend. They trust you, and may or may not tell you things that are geared towards academics all the time. It is your duty to counsel them if they have any issue that may be hindering their success rate.
